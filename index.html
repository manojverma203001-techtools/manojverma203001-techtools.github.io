Traaju App

Simple Grocery Rate & Weight Calculator

📱 About the App

Traaju App helps users easily calculate grocery prices and weights. It is designed for daily use with a simple and user-friendly interface.

⚖️ Features

1. Weight Calculator

Calculate how much quantity you get for a given amount.

  • Kilo ka bhaav (₹ per kg)
  • Khareed ki rakam (₹ amount)
  • Result (Weight in kg/grams)

Example: ₹40/kg, ₹20 → 500 grams

2. Rate Calculator

Calculate total price based on weight.

  • Kilo ka bhaav (₹ per kg)
  • Saman ka vajan (Weight)
  • Total price

Example: ₹40/kg, 500g → ₹20

3. Normal Calculator

Perform basic arithmetic calculations for daily use.
