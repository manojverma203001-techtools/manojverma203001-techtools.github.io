Traaju App

Simple Grocery Rate & Weight Calculator

📱 About the App

This app allows users to calculate grocery item rate against weight and weight against rate easily and quickly.

Download on Play Store

⚖️ Features

1. Weight Calculator

Calculate how much quantity you get for a given amount.

  • Kilo ka bhaav (₹ per kg)
  • Khareed ki rakam (₹ amount)
  • Result (Weight in kg/grams)
Example:
Price per kg: ₹40
Amount: ₹20
Result: 500 grams

2. Rate Calculator

Calculate total price based on weight.

  • Kilo ka bhaav (₹ per kg)
  • Saman ka vajan (Weight)
  • Result (Total price)
Example:
Price per kg: ₹40
Weight: 500 grams
Result: ₹20
